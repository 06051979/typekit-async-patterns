Typekit async loading pattern: Standard snippet at the bottom of the body

Takes the standard Typekit snippet and places it at the bottom of the <body> instead of the top of the <head>. This means that the browser parses the entire page before it starts downloading the Typekit fonts.

Recommendations:

  • Place this snippet at the bottom of the <body>

Advantages:

  • Loads Typekit fonts at the end of parsing the page (doesn't block page rendering while it loads)
  • Exactly the same number of bytes as the standard Typekit snippet

Disadvantages:

  • Causes a flash of unstyled text (FOUT) in all browsers that can't be controlled or hidden
  • The fonts download and display later since the download doesn't start until the browser is almost done parsing the page
